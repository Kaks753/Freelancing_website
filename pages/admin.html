How the Admin Panel Works

  • Orders are read from your Google Sheet (published as CSV or via gviz/tq JSON).
  • Make sure it's published: File → Share → Publish to web → Sheet1 → CSV → Publish.
  • To update status: click any row → use quick status buttons → opens the Sheet.
  • Click Refresh to reload after Sheet changes.
  • Change ADMIN_PASSWORD in this file to something personal.
  • This page has noindex — not in search engines.

Required Sheet Columns (Row 1)

Your Google Sheet must have these headers in row 1 (exact names):

ref  |  email  |  clientName  |  service  |  serviceShort  |  deadline  |  price  |  status  |  updated  |  message  |  file1Name  |  file1Url  |  file2Name  |  file2Url  |  milestone1  |  milestone1Done  |  milestone2  |  milestone2Done  |  milestone3  |  milestone3Done  |  milestone4  |  milestone4Done

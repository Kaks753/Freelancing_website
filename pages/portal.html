NeuroDesk Setup Instructions

One-time Google Sheets setup to power the portal. Takes 5 minutes.

  1. Go to Google Sheets → Create a new sheet called "NeuroDesk Orders"
  2. Create columns in row 1: ref | email | clientName | service | serviceShort | deadline | price | status | updated | message | file1Name | file1Url | file2Name | file2Url | milestone1 | milestone1Done | milestone2 | milestone2Done | milestone3 | milestone3Done | milestone4 | milestone4Done
  3. Go to File → Share → Publish to web → Choose "Sheet1" → Format: CSV → Click "Publish"
  4. Copy the published CSV URL and update the SHEET_URL constant in the JavaScript below
  5. Add orders row by row. Status values: Received / In Progress / Review / Done
  6. To share a deliverable file: upload to Google Drive, copy the shareable link, paste in file1Url
// Replace this URL with your published Google Sheet CSV URL:
const SHEET_URL = "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ytVRvxfhQ48lopNRANLqUnHykOUwLJxPc6laLXQDG8k/pub?output=csv";
